Bike Rental Demand Prediction

Predicting Bike Rental Demand for Capital Bikeshare — With over 8,000 bicycles across 800+ stations, Capital Bikeshare faces significant operational challenges. Inaccurate demand forecasting leads to bike shortages during peak hours, resulting in customer dissatisfaction and revenue loss, while excess idle bikes during low-demand periods drive up maintenance costs. This project addresses these challenges by developing a machine learning model to predict hourly bike rental demand, enabling data-driven decisions for inventory optimization and cost reduction.

Customer Cohort Retention Analysis.

This project performs a cohort-based retention analysis on the Superstore dataset (2014–2017) to understand customer purchasing behavior over time. Using SQL and data visualization tools, customers are grouped into cohorts based on their first purchase period, and retention rates are calculated by tracking repeat purchases across subsequent months. The analysis highlights how customer engagement declines over time but shows seasonal patterns—particularly increased retention toward the end of the year. The resulting insights help identify trends in customer loyalty and provide actionable recommendations for improving retention strategies and overall business performance.
